Learning Path

Explore the curriculum for Grades 4-7.

Grade 4 Learning Areas

In Grade 4, learners explore these key areas:

  • Numbers, Operations, and Relationships: Working with 4 and 5-digit numbers, place value, addition, subtraction, multiplication (2-digit by 2-digit), and division (3-digit by 1-digit with remainders).
  • Fractions: Recognizing common fractions (1/3, 1/4, 1/5, 1/6), comparing fractions with the same denominator, and finding fractions of whole numbers.
  • Patterns, Functions, and Algebra: Identifying rules in numeric sequences, using input/output diagrams, and recognizing geometric patterns.
  • Space and Shape (Geometry): Classifying 2D shapes (squares, rectangles, circles, pentagons, hexagons) and identifying 3D objects (spheres, prisms, pyramids), and lines of symmetry.
  • Measurement and Data Handling: Measuring length (mm, cm, m, km), understanding mass and capacity (g, kg, ml, l), and collecting data to create pictographs and bar graphs.

Grade 5 Learning Areas

In Grade 5, learners explore these key areas:

  • Whole Numbers: Extends place value to millions, focuses on multiplication and division with larger numbers, and introduces prime numbers.
  • Fractions: Focuses on equivalent fractions, comparing and ordering fractions, and adding/subtracting fractions with unlike denominators.
  • Decimals: Introduces decimal place value, converting between decimals and fractions, and performing operations with decimals.
  • 2D Geometry: Measuring and classifying angles, focuses on area and perimeter of various polygons.
  • Data Handling: Focuses on collecting, organizing, and interpreting data using various types of graphs.

Grade 6 Learning Areas

In Grade 6, learners explore these key areas:

  • Integers: Introduces negative numbers, comparing/ordering integers, and performing integer operations.
  • Ratio and Proportion: Introduces ratios, rates, proportions, and their applications in real-world problems.
  • Algebra: Introduces algebraic expressions and solving simple equations.
  • 3D Geometry: Focuses on surface area and volume of rectangular prisms and cylinders.
  • Data Handling: Introduces measures of central tendency (mean, median, mode) and data representation with histograms.

Grade 7 Learning Areas

In Grade 7, learners explore these key areas:

  • Integers and Rational Numbers: Extending operations to include all rational numbers (fractions, decimals, percentages).
  • Algebra: Simplifying expressions, solving equations and inequalities, and graphing linear equations.
  • Geometry: Exploring angle relationships, similar figures, and the Pythagorean theorem.
  • Measurement: Calculating area and circumference of circles, and volume of prisms and cylinders.
  • Probability and Statistics: Understanding experimental vs. theoretical probability, and data analysis with scatter plots.
